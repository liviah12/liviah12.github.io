About Me

Hi! My name is Olivia Hebert. I am currently a junior at the University of Colorado Boulder pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a minor in Creative Technology and Design. Through my courses and schoolwork I have learned C++, HTML/CS, JavaScript, Python, SQL and utilized design tools such as Figma, Adobe suite, and Canva.

I currently work at MagicSchool AI as an intern where I have worked on a variety of projects including data visualization using SQL and Metabase, designing and developing their website, and utilizing Canva and Adobe on various design projects. My time at MagicSchool, working in a collaborative and passionate environment, has fostered a desire to develop innovative technical solutions that enhance people’s daily lives. MagicSchool is a mission driven organization specializing in AI for teachers and their commitment to improving the lives of teachers is inspiring and has shown me just how life changing technology can be.

In addition to utilizing technology to support people I also am interested in the intersection of technology and creativity. I love the more “creative” side of technology such as web development, product design, and user experience and interaction.

At my core I am an avid learner and I look forward to forever be learning due to the constantly evolving nature of computer science, technology, and design trends.
